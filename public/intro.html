Episode I

REVENGE OF CODERS


The FIRST ORDER reigns. Having decimated the peaceful Republic, Supreme Leader PLAYER 1 now deploys the merciless legions to seize military control of the galaxy.
Only General PLAYER 2's band of RESISTANCE fighters stand against the rising tyranny, certain that Jedi Master CJ Hannah will return and restore a spark of code to the fight.
But the Resistance has been exposed.
Rebel spaceships, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the evil Galactic Empire.
During the battle, Rebel spies managed to steal secret plans to the Empire's ultimate weapon, the DEATH STAR, an armored space station with enough power to destroy an entire planet.
Pursued by the Empire's sinister agents, PLAYER 2 races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her people and restore freedom to the galaxy.....
